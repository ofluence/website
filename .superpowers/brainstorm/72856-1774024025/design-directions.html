A — "Midnight Linear"

Dark-first, sharp, professional. Deep navy/black backgrounds with indigo accent gradients. Tight geometric grid. Monospace details. Minimal but powerful animations — opacity fades and subtle transforms. Think Linear meets Raycast.

B — "Warm Kinetic" (Recommended)

Light-first with warm energy. Cream/stone base with orange-red gradients as the hero accent. Rounded shapes, generous spacing. Playful motion — staggered reveals, morphing shapes, interactive hover states. Think Framer meets Lemon Squeezy. Feels approachable for creators while still sharp enough for enterprise.

C — "Neon Slate"

Dark mode with electric accents. Slate blue backgrounds with cyan/purple gradient highlights. Grid overlays, subtle glow effects, data-viz energy. Technical and modern — like Vercel's boldness applied to marketing. Great for brands/agencies, may feel less approachable for creators.
