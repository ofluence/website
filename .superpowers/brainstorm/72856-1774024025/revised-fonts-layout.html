What do you prefer?

Stacked Center

Everything centered and stacked vertically. Headline → subtext → CTAs → full-width product below. Clean, Apple/Linear-style. Maximizes product visibility.

Full-Bleed Container

Hero lives inside a full-bleed rounded container/card. Text over subtle background texture or pattern. The container itself creates visual drama. Think Stripe's floating card aesthetic.
