Complete Design Summary

Full redesign spec — "Warm Kinetic" direction for Ofluence

Design Identity: "Warm Kinetic"

Bold, energetic, modern. Light-first with warm accents. Rounded shapes, spring-based motion, interactive product showcases. Bridges the gap between enterprise credibility and creator-culture energy. Inspired by Linear.app, Framer.com, and modash.io.

Typography

Display: Sora — 800 (hero), 700 (section), 600 (subsection)
Body: Geist Sans — 400/450/500
Hero scale: clamp(36px, 6vw, 64px), -2.5px tracking
Overlines: Geist 600, 12px, uppercase, 2px tracking, orange

Colors

Light BG: #fafaf8 warm white
Dark BG: #0c0c0c true dark
Accent: #f97316 solid orange
Gradients: Radial glows only, ≤6% opacity
Surface: #f5f5f0 (light) / #161616 (dark)

Layout

Hero: Full-bleed rounded container, centered
Features: Asymmetric bento grid (2 large + 2 std)
Spacing: py-20 / py-28 balanced
Max width: 1280px content container
Radius: 20px containers, 16px cards, 10px buttons

Motion

Engine: Spring physics (stiffness: 100, damping: 15)
Entry: Fade-in + rise on section load, staggered
Hover: Card lift (-4px) + shadow + border glow
Ambient: Radial glow drift, dot grid texture
Tabs: Layout-animated indicator, cross-fade content
